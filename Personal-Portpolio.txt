                <h3><b>ABOUT ME</b></h3>
                <p>
                    I am John Philip Miguel T. Lorenzo, a dedicated student leader and chess enthusiast currently serving as the Protocol Officer for STEM Alliance. With a strong passion for strategic thinking and community engagement, I have actively participated in various school organizations and competitions throughout my academic journey.
                </p>
                
                <p>
                    My leadership experience spans multiple roles including Class Treasurer, where I managed financial responsibilities, and various positions in student organizations such as Yes-O Organization, BKD, and Boy Scout of the Philippines. These experiences have shaped my ability to work collaboratively and contribute meaningfully to team efforts.
                </p>
                
                <p>
                    Beyond academics, I have demonstrated excellence in competitive chess, earning multiple championship titles at intramural and division levels. My participation in the Central Luzon Regional Athletic Association (CLRAA) Chess Competition reflects my commitment to representing my school at higher competitive levels.
                </p>
                
                <p>
                    Through my involvement in technical committees and working groups for various school programs, I have developed strong organizational and coordination skills. I believe in the power of student leadership to create positive change and meaningful experiences within our educational community.
                </p>
